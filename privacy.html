Privacy Policy for Reaction Bridge

Last Updated: 02.09.2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Reaction Bridge" browser extension handles your information. Our core principle is to collect and store the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our service.

Information We Access (via Google/YouTube API)

To function, Reaction Bridge requires your permission to access certain YouTube features on your behalf. When you authenticate with your Google Account, we use the official YouTube Data API to perform actions that you initiate. This includes:

  • Viewing your YouTube account: To identify your active channel.
  • Managing your YouTube account: To perform actions like liking/disliking a video, subscribing/unsubscribing to a channel, and posting/editing/deleting your comments when you explicitly click the corresponding buttons in the extension.

Information We Do Not Collect or Store

We do not store any of your personal data on our own servers. All interactions with the YouTube API happen directly from your browser to Google's servers. We do not see, log, or store:

  • Your Google password.
  • Your personal email address.
  • Your browsing history.
  • Any personal information from your Google or YouTube profile.

The only data stored is kept locally in your browser's storage (e.g., the ID of a comment you posted via the extension, so you can manage it later). This data never leaves your computer.

How to Revoke Access

You are in full control. You can revoke the extension's access to your YouTube account at any time by visiting your Google Account permissions page and removing "Reaction Bridge" from the list of third-party apps with account access.
